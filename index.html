The Manifesto

Rooted in history,
steeped in culture.

Virasat translates to heritage. We exist as custodians of the authentic kadak brew — refusing commercial shortcuts, sourcing exclusively from heritage estates to preserve the rich, robust flavour profiles that defined an era of tea craftsmanship.

Every leaf is hand-picked at peak maturity. Every spice is stone-ground using traditional methods. We don't just make tea — we preserve a ritual that has united families across generations.

The Ritual

How we brew
perfection.

01

Source & Select

Hand-selected leaves from Assam and Darjeeling heritage estates. Only the top two leaves and a bud make the cut.

02

Stone Grind Spices

Cardamom, ginger, cloves and cinnamon — stone-ground fresh for each micro-batch. No pre-processed powders, ever.

03

Slow Simmer

Brewed low-and-slow on traditional brass vessels. The slow extraction pulls maximum flavour from every ingredient.

04

Seal & Ship

Nitrogen-flushed packaging within 48 hours of roasting. Arrives at your doorstep with peak freshness locked in.

Know Your Brew

The purest botanicals,
carefully selected.

Kashmiri Saffron

Sourced from elevated terrains at 1,600m altitude, our saffron comes exclusively from Pampore, Kashmir — the only place in India where this precious spice grows. Each strand is hand-picked at dawn when the flowers first bloom.

RegionPampore, Kashmir
Altitude1,600m
HarvestOctober — November
GradeMongra (Grade I)

Assam Orthodox

Full-bodied black tea cultivated in the region's most historic estates along the Brahmaputra valley. Our orthodox-process leaves are rolled and fermented slowly to unlock complex malty flavours impossible with CTC methods.

RegionUpper Assam
Altitude45-120m
HarvestSecond Flush (June)
ProcessOrthodox Roll

Malabar Cardamom

Intensely aromatic green pods from the Cardamom Hills of Kerala. We use only 8mm+ grade extra-bold pods, cracked open moments before blending to preserve volatile essential oils that give our chai its signature aroma.

RegionIdukki, Kerala
Altitude600-1,500m
Grade8mm+ Extra Bold
MethodCracked Fresh

Wayanad Ginger

Wild-harvested ginger from the Wayanad forests of northern Kerala. Higher in gingerol content than commercial varieties, it provides the characteristic sharp warmth that cuts through milk and sugar perfectly.

RegionWayanad, Kerala
TypeWild-Harvested
ProfileHigh Gingerol
MethodSun-Dried Slices
